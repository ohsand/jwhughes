About Our Firm

J W Hughes & Co are a firm of solicitors with offices in Llandudno and Conwy on the North Wales coast. J W Hughes & Co Solicitors was originally established in 1903. We have a great team of people behind us offering the best advice in all aspects of Law.

At JW Hughes, we believe in the power of collaboration, and we work tirelessly to build strong relationships with our clients based on trust, integrity, and mutual respect. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in everything we do, from our rigorous attention to detail to our unwavering dedication to achieving the best possible outcomes for our clients.

Practice Areas

Our team of experienced attorneys specializes in a wide range of practice areas. We take a personalized approach to every case, working closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and develop customized solutions that are tailored to their specific circumstances.

Criminal Law

Our experienced attorneys handle a wide range of criminal matters, including both misdemeanor and felony charges.

Real Estate Law

We provide comprehensive legal advice and representation to both individuals and businesses in all aspects of real estate law.

Family Law

We handle a wide range of family law issues, including divorce, child custody, child support, spousal support, and property division.

International Law

We advise clients on a wide range of international law matters, including international trade, investment, cross-border transactions, and disputes resolution.

Financial Law

Our team is dedicated to helping our clients navigate the ever-changing financial regulatory landscape, while minimizing risks and ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

Technology Law

We work closely with our clients to develop customized legal strategies to help with protecting intellectual property, drafting technology contracts, or ensuring compliance with data privacy regulations.
